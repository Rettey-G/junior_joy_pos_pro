Basic Operations

To create a new sale:

  1. Navigate to the "New Sale" page from the navigation menu
  2. Enter the customer name
  3. Search for products using the search box
  4. Click on products to add them to the cart
  5. Adjust quantities as needed
  6. Apply any discounts
  7. Select payment method
  8. Enter amount paid
  9. Click "Create Invoice" to complete the sale

Customer Service Excellence

Building Customer Loyalty

Loyal customers spend 67% more than new customers. Here's how to build lasting relationships:

  • Personalized Service - Use the Junior Joy POS customer database to remember preferences and purchase history
  • Loyalty Programs - Implement points systems and special offers for repeat customers
  • Follow-up Communication - Send thank you emails and personalized offers
  • Problem Resolution - Turn complaints into opportunities by exceeding expectations

Handling Difficult Situations

Follow the LAST method for resolving customer complaints:

  • Listen - Give the customer your full attention
  • Acknowledge - Validate their concerns
  • Solve - Provide a solution that exceeds expectations
  • Thank - Express appreciation for their feedback

Sales Techniques for Retail Success

The SELL Method

  • Smile and greet every customer warmly
  • Engage by asking open-ended questions about their needs
  • Listen actively to understand their true requirements
  • Lead them to the right solution with your product knowledge

Upselling and Cross-selling

Increase your average transaction value with these techniques:

  • Upselling - Suggest premium versions of products customers are interested in
  • Cross-selling - Recommend complementary products that enhance their purchase
  • Bundle Offers - Create special packages that provide value and increase sales

Closing Techniques

Effective ways to guide customers to a purchase decision:

  • Assumptive Close - "Would you like me to gift wrap this for you?"
  • Choice Close - "Would you prefer the blue one or the red one?"
  • Urgency Close - "This is the last one in stock at this price"
  • Summary Close - Recap the benefits before asking for the sale

Troubleshooting

Common System Issues

  • 404 Page Not Found: If you encounter a 404 error, please return to the main application and try again.
  • Failed to Save Customer: Ensure all required fields are filled correctly.
  • Connection Issues: Check your internet connection and refresh the page.
  • Printing Problems: Verify printer connection and settings in your browser.
